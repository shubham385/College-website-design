India's No.1 University

The University of Mumbai, informally known as Mumbai University, is a collegiate public state university located in the city of Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. The University of Mumbai is one of the largest universities in the world.
The University of Mumbai was ranked 89 in QS BRICS 2019 ranking and 351 in QS WUR Subject ranking 2020.
